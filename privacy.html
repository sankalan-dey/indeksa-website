Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Thank you for using Indeksa. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights when using the application.

1. Information We Access

Indeksa requests permission to access your screenshots so that it can index and search them.

Your screenshots remain on your device.

We do not upload your screenshots or their contents to our own servers.

2. OCR Processing

Text recognition (OCR) is performed locally on your device.

The extracted text is stored only on your device to enable fast searching.

We do not receive or store the text extracted from your screenshots.

3. Analytics

Indeksa may use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as:

  • App launches
  • Feature usage
  • Device model
  • Android version
  • General diagnostic information

Analytics data does not include your screenshots or OCR text.

4. Crash Reporting

Google Firebase Crashlytics may collect anonymous crash reports and technical diagnostics to improve app stability.

5. Permissions

Indeksa may request access to:

  • Photos and Media

This permission is used solely to locate, index, and search screenshots stored on your device.

6. Data Storage

All indexed data remains stored locally on your device.

We do not maintain servers that store your screenshots or extracted text.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics

These services are governed by Google's own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Indeksa is not intended specifically for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective once published on this page.
